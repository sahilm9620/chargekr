Play Store & AI model optimization notes

Play Store listing essentials

  • App title: ChargeKr (keep exact brand casing)
  • Short description: 80 characters highlighting P2P charger sharing and earnings
  • Graphics: feature graphic, screenshots, app icon
  • Privacy & data: link to privacy policy
  • Package: com.chargekr (do NOT change)

AI model / assistant readiness

  • Ensure `llms.txt` is included in `sitemap.xml` and allowed in `robots.txt`
  • Use clear JSON-LD on pages (Organization, WebSite, SoftwareApplication, FAQPage)
  • OSRM calls: include custom User-Agent to avoid 403s

Next steps

  • Run structured-data tests for pages with `FAQPage` and `SoftwareApplication` JSON-LD
  • Track Search Console and Play Console errors after publishing changes
