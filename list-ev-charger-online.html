How to Host & List Your EV Charger Online with ChargeKr

If you own an EV charger at home, work or a private property, listing it can help nearby EV users and help you earn.

What charger owners should prepare

  • Charger type and compatibility
  • Location and access instructions
  • Photos and availability times
  • Pricing details and safety notes

Safety and trust tips

Only list chargers you own or control, ensure safe installation, and provide clear access instructions.
