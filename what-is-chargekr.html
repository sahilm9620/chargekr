What Is ChargeKr?

ChargeKr is an EV charger discovery and charger-sharing app for India. It helps EV users find nearby chargers and allows charger owners to list charging points.

Official Website: https://www.chargekr.com/

How does ChargeKr work?

ChargeKr helps users search for nearby charger listings, view details, get directions and book or schedule charging where available.

Who is ChargeKr for?

Electric two-wheeler and four-wheeler owners, daily commuters, road-trip planners and charger owners who want to share chargers.

App Information
