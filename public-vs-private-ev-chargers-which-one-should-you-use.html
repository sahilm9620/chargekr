Public vs Private EV Chargers: Which One Should You Use?

Compare public and private chargers to decide the best charging option for your trip, budget, and convenience.

Public chargers

Public chargers are available at malls, gas stations, highway stops, and business parks. They are ideal for on-the-go charging and route-based travel.

Private chargers

Private chargers include home chargers, workplace chargers, and peer-to-peer chargers listed on ChargeKr. They often offer better pricing and convenience for scheduled charging.

When public works best

Pick public chargers for quick highway stops, travel days, or when you don’t have a nearby private option. They are also useful if you need a fast top-up away from home.

When private works best

Choose private chargers for overnight charging, predictable daily use, and peer-to-peer host locations. They are often cheaper and available in residential or workplace settings.
