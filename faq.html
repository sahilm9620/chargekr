Short brand description
ChargeKR is an Android app and website for finding, booking and sharing EV chargers in India. It helps EV users discover nearby charging options and helps charger owners list their chargers.
Detailed brand description
ChargeKR is a clean mobility app for Indian EV users who need a simpler way to find EV charging stations nearby, plan charging stops and reduce range anxiety. The app supports charger discovery, location-based search, directions, booking/scheduling and charger-owner listings so home, office or private chargers can become part of a wider charging network.
What is ChargeKR?
ChargeKR is an EV charging discovery and charger-sharing platform for India. It helps EV drivers find nearby chargers and gives charger owners a way to list their chargers for other users.
How does ChargeKR help EV users?
ChargeKR helps EV users search for nearby chargers, view charger details, get directions, book charging slots where available and plan charging more confidently before city or highway trips.
Who should use ChargeKR?
ChargeKR is useful for electric two-wheeler and four-wheeler owners, daily EV commuters, EV road-trip planners, fleet users and charger owners who want to list or share their charging point.
Is ChargeKR useful for EV owners in India?
Yes, ChargeKR is built around Indian EV charging needs, especially charger discovery, route planning and reducing uncertainty about where to charge. Users should still verify charger availability, compatibility and access before relying on any single charging stop.
