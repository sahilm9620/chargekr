About Us

Powering the Future of EV Charging

We're on a mission to make EV charging accessible, affordable, and community-driven — one shared charger at a time.

Our Story

Born from a Real Problem

Chargekr was born out of a simple frustration: the lack of accessible EV charging infrastructure in India. As EV adoption surges, the charging network hasn't kept pace. Thousands of EV owners experience range anxiety daily, while millions of home chargers sit idle for 20+ hours a day.

We saw a gap that could be bridged — not by waiting for governments or corporations to build more stations, but by empowering communities to share what they already have.

Founded in 2025, Chargekr is India's first peer-to-peer EV charging platform. We connect EV drivers with nearby charger owners, creating a decentralized, community-powered charging network that benefits everyone — drivers, hosts, and the planet.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To democratize EV charging by building a peer-to-peer network that makes charging accessible, affordable, and convenient for every EV owner in India.

Our Vision

A world where every EV charger is a shared resource — eliminating range anxiety, reducing carbon emissions, and empowering communities to lead the clean energy transition.

Our Values

Community first. Sustainability always. Transparency in everything we do. Innovation that serves people, not just profits.

What We Do

The Chargekr Platform

We build technology that connects people and powers sustainable mobility.

Mobile App

Our apps for Android and iOS let EV drivers discover, book, and pay for charging sessions at shared chargers near them. Hosts can list, manage, and monetize their chargers with a few taps.

Smart Charger Network

Our AI-powered platform maps every shared charger, predicts availability, and recommends the best charging options based on route, vehicle type, and user preferences.

Secure Payments

End-to-end encrypted payments processed through trusted payment gateways. Hosts receive automatic weekly payouts directly to their bank accounts.

Trust & Safety

Every user and charger is verified. Host protection insurance covers damages up to ₹2,00,000. Our 24/7 support team ensures every session is safe and smooth.
