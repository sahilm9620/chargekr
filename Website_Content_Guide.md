# ChargeKr: India's First Peer-to-Peer EV Charging Platform
**Website Content & Feature Guide for Web Developers**

## 1. Hero Section & Core Identity
- **App Name:** ChargeKr
- **Tagline:** Share your charger, earn money, build a greener future.
- **The Concept:** Think "Airbnb for EV Chargers." ChargeKr is a peer-to-peer EV charging network that bridges the gap in EV infrastructure. It allows EV owners to naturally find affordable, neighborhood-level chargers while empowering charger owners (Hosts) to monetize their idle charging stations.
- **Design Vibe (For the Web Dev):** Cinematic, premium, Apple-inspired aesthetics. Use Deep/Pure Blacks (#000000), stark high-contrast typography, soft & pill-shaped elements (generous border-radius), and vivid accents leveraging "Apple System Blue" (#0071E3). The website should feel modern, sleek, and highly trustworthy.

---

## 2. Value Proposition for EV Drivers 🚗 (Find & Charge)
**Headline Idea:** Never Worry About Range Anxiety Again. Find reliable chargers in your neighborhood.

**Key Features to Highlight:**
* **Interactive Map & Smart Filters:** Automatically locates nearby chargers based on a live dynamic radius. Filter by Charger Type (Level 1, Level 2, DC Fast), Connector Type, Distance, Pricing, and Ratings.
* **Seamless Booking & Scheduling:** "Book Now" for instant charging, or schedule a slot days in advance. Pick optimal charging durations specific to your battery needs.
* **Live Session Tracking:** View real-time charging progress, time remaining, and estimated costs via active session monitoring.
* **Transparent Details & Reviews:** Every charger profile shows exact hourly rates, verified access instructions, community reviews, and high-quality photos.
* **Integrated Navigation:** Quick-launch into map apps to navigate straight to the host's driveway or parking spot.

---

## 3. Value Proposition for Hosts ⚡️ (Share & Earn)
**Headline Idea:** Turn Your Parking Spot into Passive Income. Earn up to ₹15,000/month.

**Key Features to Highlight:**
* **Easy and Secure Listing:** Add your charger in minutes. Input power output, set up photos, specify connector types, and provide safe access instructions.
* **AI-Suggested Pricing & Custom Rates:** Use ChargeKr's smart AI to set the perfect hourly rate for your neighborhood, or take manual control over your pricing.
* **Complete Schedule Control:** Define your exact availability windows. Block out times when you need the charger for your own vehicle.
* **Host Dashboard & Earnings Tracking:** Monitor your total earnings, active sessions, and upcoming bookings at a glance. Track performance by "This Week" or "This Month".
* **Approval Authority:** Freedom to choose who charges. You can "Approve" or "Decline" pending booking requests.

---

## 4. Platform Trust & Safety 🔒
* **KYC Verified Community:** Both drivers and hosts go through a rigorous KYC (Know Your Customer) identity verification to ensure absolute platform safety.
* **OTP Verified Charging:** Secure start/stop process using One-Time Passwords directly exchanged between Driver and Host to prevent unauthorized sessions.
* **Secure Payments:** Hassle-free digital wallets and payment history tracking.

---

## 5. Technical Callouts (If Needed for FAQ/Tech Section)
* **Platform:** Currently available for Android (Download via Google Play Store).
* **Architecture:** Offline-first capable App interactions using Android Compose & Room caching with real-time Spring Boot backend sync.
* **Dark Mode Native:** Designed ground-up for OLED screens providing maximum battery saving and visual comfort.
