Best EV Charging Apps in India

Compare the top apps that help EV drivers find chargers, reserve slots, and review charger locations across India.

Why the right app matters

In India, EV charging is evolving quickly. The best EV charging apps combine real-time station data with route planning, charger filters, and user feedback. The goal is simple: find a charger near you, choose the right connector, and avoid long wait times.

Top app features for EV drivers

Nearby charger search: Locate chargers within minutes of your current position.
Fast charger filters: Only see fast or DC fast chargers when you need a quick top-up.
User reviews: Read feedback from other drivers before you arrive.
Slot booking: Reserve your charging time ahead for stress-free charging.

How ChargeKr stands out

ChargeKr brings both public and peer-to-peer chargers into one map. That means you can discover shared home and business chargers in addition to commercial stations. In cities like Pune and Mumbai, this expands your charging options significantly.

ChargeKr also helps drivers compare prices, confirm availability, and see reviews for listed chargers.

Best use cases

City commuting: Find chargers near office hubs, malls, and apartment complexes.
Road trips: Plan stops with fast chargers on highway corridors.
Charger review checks: Choose chargers with high ratings and reliable availability.
