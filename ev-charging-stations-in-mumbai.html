Mumbai charging overview

Mumbai is growing its charging infrastructure rapidly, especially in South Mumbai, Powai, Andheri, and Navi Mumbai. Public chargers are now available at malls, offices, and transit hubs, while shared chargers add flexibility for local drivers.

Fast charging in the city

Fast chargers are the best option when you need a quick top-up. Search for DC fast stations near highways or business districts, and use ChargeKR to compare power levels and user reviews.

Charger reliability

ChargeKR helps you choose reliable Mumbai chargers by showing availability updates and ratings. Pick chargers with frequent positive reviews and clear instructions to avoid downtime.

Where to charge in Mumbai

Malls and retail parks: Good for shopping or leisure while charging.
Office areas: Convenient for daytime charging near workplaces.
Residential chargers: Shared options can be great for local drivers and overnight charging.
