EV Charging Etiquette: What Every EV Owner Should Know

Follow the best practices that keep shared and public EV charging convenient and respectful for everyone.

Arrive on time

If you reserve a charger slot, arrive within the booked window. Timely arrival helps hosts and other drivers stay on schedule.

Keep the area clean

After charging, clear any trash and return cables neatly. A clean charging area makes the experience better for the next driver.

Follow host instructions

Use the charger exactly as the host describes. If there are special entry instructions or connector notes, follow them carefully.

Respect shared charging

Shared chargers are a community resource. Don’t overstay your booked time, and make future bookings only when you genuinely need the charger.
